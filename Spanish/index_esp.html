RSP Opinion: The U.S. is on the road to fascism

Written by Noah Hoffman

Why social Democracy is not for the proletariat


Some leftists (and far from socialists) will argue that social-democracy is sufficient as an ideology and that reforming capitalism with welfare and high taxes is a sufficient system. Us Marxists however know this is not the case! Social-Democracy (despite the convincing name) is not socialist at all and therefore does not stand with the proletariat and instead betrays them. Social-Democracy is still capitalism; of which in of itself is inherently flawed. Social-Democracy still advocates for the dictatorship of the bourgeois and still keeps the 1% of richest people in the world in power over the 99% of workers and citizens. Those who advocate for such a system are either blinded by bourgeois lies or are bourgeois themself! Such a system still relies on exploitative labor of poor citizens in third-world nations; We do not agree with this! We don't believe in taxes or exploitation of foreign citizens' labor for profit, we believe in people and the planet before profits. Social-Democracy is like taking a decrepit house and painting it to make it look better. Sure, the place looks better, but it's still a decrepit decaying building; you didn't solve the root issue. This is exactly the problem with Social-Democracy! It is capitalism painted to look "marginally" better, however all the faults and inevitably exploitative properties of capitalism are still immensely present. As such, anyone who actively advocates for Social-Democracy cannot be called a true Socialist or revolutionary for the proletariat and instead are betrayers of the workers.

Once us workers organize and become strong enough, those in power will start to fear over our might. Because of this, they may try and offer concessions such as Social-Democracy. Us workers however, must not fall into the trap of beliving the bourgeois concessions; we won't stop there! We shall continue to fight until the proletariat seize the means of production.

Workers of our nation, organize! Join and support local unions and Marxist parties. Do not let the bourgeois persist in their exploitative ways for we will stop at nothing until us workers have won. So workers of the world, unite! You have nothing to loose but your chains!


I'm lost. How can I learn more about socialism?

The Marxist Internet Archive!

The Marxist Internet Archive is the best online site for socialist resources preserved in their original forms online for free for anyone to view. If you want to learn more about Socialism then this is the place to be! We strongly encourage you to use this site as it is immensly helpful for accessing socialist works in this modern era.

Socialism101!

Socialism101 is another great site with a fantastic reading list containing books for beggining Socialists to read. It also has an amazing, descriptive, and very easy to understand page explaining Socialism in a very sound format.

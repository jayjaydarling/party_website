Vladimir Lenin

"There are decades where nothing happens; and there are weeks where decades happen."
-Vladimir Lenin

Born: 22 April 1870
Died: 21 January 1924
Chairman of the Council of People's Commissars of the USSR: 1923-1924
Established the Bolshevik party
Expanded onto Marxist theory (i.e., the state and the vanguard party)
First person in history to establish a workers state!

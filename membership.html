Membership


So you want to join the Revolutionary Students' Party?

Great! That's nice to hear. If you wish to join the Revolutionary Students' Party then you must fill out two forms. One form is digital and the other physical.

The digital form can be found here.

The physical form can be found here (PDF)

Please make sure to fill out both forms. Once you fill out and submit the digital form you are done with the digital one. Once you finish filling out the physical one you must save the filled out copy of the PDF and email it to tomkelpfish@gmail.com. If you recieved a copy of the physical form in person, then please return it filled out to the official that you recieved it from. If you no longer have access to the official that you recieved it from or have any way to contact any other officials, then please either scan or take quality photos of the entire filled out form and email them to tomkelpfish@gmail.com

Your submission will be evaluated as soon as possible and you will contacted regarding your acceptance. For more information please refer to the physical copy of the application. You can email tomkelpfish@gmail.com with any relevent questions.

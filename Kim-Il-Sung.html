The Communists had won the elections (democratically) and out of fear the Americans had immediantly abolished the elections and had an American military dictatorship set up in the south. So much for "freedom" and "democracy" that the U.S. stands for. Kim-Il Sung (Korean: 김일성) was a politician, orator, writer, guerilla soldier, general, president, and is the Eternal Leader of Korea. Kim-Il Sung served as the president of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea (DPRK; North Korea) from 1972 to 1994. He is honored in the DPRK, (secretly) in South Korea, and all over the world by workers alike for his commitment towards liberating Korea and her people.

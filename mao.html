Did you know...

Quotations from Chairman Mao Zedong (The Little Red Book) is one of the most published and distributed books in the world? Alongside the bible, Quotations from Chairman Mao Zedong has been published roughly 900 million times and is one of the top most distributed books ever in history.

originally made for PLA (People's Liberation Army; the military of the People's Republic of China), Quoations from Chairman Mao Zedong was published for the Chinese public in 1964. The book was mass published with the intent of having every single Chinese Citizen have possesion of one. The book contains writtings, speeches, and letters from Mao Zedong covering various topics that affect everyday Chinese life from a Marxist perspective.

Quoations from Chairman Mao Zedong
The book is still sold in China today.

Mao Zedong (Chinese: 毛泽东) was a politician, orator, writer, military stratigist, Chairman, and developed Maoism and Mao Zedong Thought through his writtings and practices of applying Marxism to China. He is honored all over the world (and especially in the global south) for his commitment towards devloping Maoism and being an inspirational figure for communists.

Communist Party of China turns 100

In 2021 the Communist Party of China turned 100 years old since its founding in 1921. Chinese citizens celebrate the event in Beijing.

In 2021 the Communist Party of China (CPC) turned 100 years old. The event was celebrated all over China with special and immense celebration in Beijing. Lots of patriotic and historical songs were performed by the People's Liberation Army, and an all day stage performance called "Epic Journey" was performed depicting the modern history of china from the Chinese Civil War and the 1914 revolution to present day China. Epic Journey shows through a staged performance with singing and choreography China's struggle towards Socialism by the Chinese people.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=K6hOKvSmdIE

Over the course of a centuary the Communist party of China has been able to lead the People's Republic of China thorugh modernization where she has the second biggest GDP in the world, a flourishing healthcare system, some of the most contemporary and developed technology in the world, and the largest military in the world. After the People's Republic of China was proclaimed on October 1st 1949 and the Kuomintang regime had fallen, the Chinese people celebrated their achievement of overthrowing capitalismn and imperialism and establishing Socialism in mainland Chian. The efforts made by Chinese people during the Chinese Civil war still play a significant part in how China is today.
